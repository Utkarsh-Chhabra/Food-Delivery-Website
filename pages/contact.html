Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM, 7 days a week. Our restaurant partners may have different operating hours.

How long does delivery take?

Our average delivery time is 30 minutes, but it may vary based on your location, weather conditions, and restaurant preparation time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer full refunds for cancelled orders and partial refunds for quality issues. Please contact our support team for assistance.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through our website or by calling our customer support. You'll receive SMS updates as well.

Can I schedule a delivery for later?

Yes, you can schedule your delivery up to 7 days in advance. Select your preferred time slot during checkout.
